Getting started with 10ten Japanese Reader

  • Open Safari

  • Select AA in the address bar

    Select the extensions icon in the address bar

  • Manage Extensions

  • Enable 10ten Japanese Reader

  • Activate 10ten Japanese Reader

  • Drag the 10. circle to look up words

  • Advanced usage

    1. Tap the circle to activate and de-activate it

    2. Double-tap the circle to change its direction

You can turn on 10ten Japanese Reader’s extension in Safari Extensions preferences.
